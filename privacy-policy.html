Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

We at Calvoro are dedicated to safeguarding the confidentiality and integrity of the personal data of our website visitors. This privacy statement describes how we gather, utilize, and safeguard your information.

Personal information we collect.

When we talk about "personal information," we mean data that may be used to identify you or another individual. Information that is gathered inadvertently or that has been anonymously processed so that it cannot be used to identify or fairly be connected to you is not considered personal information. Depending on how you use the Services, where you reside, and as allowed or mandated by applicable legislation, we may gather or handle the following types of private details, including inferences made from this information:

  1. Your name, address, phone number, email address, billing address, and shipping address are all included in your contact information.
  2. Credit card, debit card, and financial account numbers, payment card information, financial the details of the account, transaction details, payment method, payment confirmation, and other payment information are examples of financial data.
  3. Your username, password, security questions, preferences, and settings are all part of your account information.
  4. The goods you examine, add to your basket, add to your wishlist, buy, return, exchange, or cancel, as well as your previous transactions, are all included in the transaction information.
  5. Communications with us, such as the details you provide when submitting a customer support query, contain information about you.
  6. Device information includes your IP address, other unique identifiers, and details about your device, browser, or network connection.
  7. Usage data includes details on how and when you use the services, as well as information about how you interact with them.

How We Use Your Personal Information

We may use your specific data for the following purposes, depending on how you engage with us or whose of the offerings you use:

  1. Deliver, customize, and enhance the services. To fulfill our obligations under our contract with you, process payments, fulfill orders, remember your preferences and items of interest, send you account-related notifications, process purchases, returns, exchanges, or other transactions, create, maintain, and otherwise manage your account, arrange shipping, facilitate returns and exchanges, allow you to post reviews, and create a personalized shopping experience for you, including suggesting products related to your purchases, we use your personal information. This might entail utilizing your personal data to further customize and enhance the services.
  2. Marketing and Promotion. In order to send you marketing, advertising, and promotional communications via email, text message, or postal mail, as well as to display online ads for products and solutions on the Services as well as other websites, including on the basis of items you have previously bought or added to your cart and other behavior on the Services, we use the information you provide to us.
  3. Fraud prevention and security. In order to safeguard public safety, provide a safe payment and shopping experience, identify, look into, or take action against potential fraudulent, unlawful, dangerous, or malicious activities, verify your account, and secure our services, we need your personal information. You are in charge of protecting your account credentials if you decide to use the Services and create an account. It is strongly advised that you keep your password, username, and any access information private.
  4. For legal reasons. We use your personal information to enforce or look into possible violations of our terms or policies, to comply with applicable law, to respond to legitimate legal process, including requests from government agencies or law enforcement, to investigate or take part in civil research, hypothetical or actual cases, or other aggressive legal proceedings.

Protection and Preservation of Your Data

Because no security mechanism is flawless or impenetrable, we are unable to provide "perfect security." Furthermore, any data you submit us could not be safe during transmission. We advise against sending critical or private information to us over insecure means.

The length of time we keep your personal data varies depending on a number of circumstances, including whether we need it to keep your account active, deliver services to you, meet legal requirements, settle disputes, or enforce any relevant agreements and rules.

Your Rights

You are entitled to request that your personal information be accessed, corrected, or deleted. Additionally, you may click the unsubscribe link in our marketing communications at any time to stop receiving them.

Complaints

Please use the information below to get in touch with us if you have any issues regarding the way we handle your personal data. Depending on where you live, you might be able to file a complaint with your local data protection body or appeal our decision by getting in touch with us using the information below.

International Transfers

Please be aware that your personal information may be processed, stored, and transferred outside of your home country.

Unless the data transfer is to a nation that has been found to offer an adequate level of protection, we will rely on established transfer mechanisms such as the European Commission's contractual standards or any equivalent contracts issued by the appropriate government agency of the UK if we transfer your personal information outside of the European Economic Area or the Kingdom of England.
