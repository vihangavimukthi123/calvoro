How do i keep my Calvoro Merch in good condition?

Observe the care label's directions. The bucket symbol will either have dots or digits inside it if the clothing is machine washable. We advise you to spin at a low speed, rinse quickly, and wash inside out at a low temperature. Avoid tumble drying or dry cleaning. Avoid ironing on prints and use a moderate temperature. Leather goods, shoes, and accessories cannot be washed.
